How to Find Your Solana Address

Your Solana wallet address can be found in your Solana wallet app or browser extension:

  1. Open your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
  2. Look for "Wallet Address" or a similar option
  3. Copy the address (usually looks like AaBb...XxYy)
  4. Paste it into the form above
Make sure you're using a wallet address you control, not a smart contract or exchange deposit address.
